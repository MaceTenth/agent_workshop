Models don't read characters — they read tokens: chunks produced by a byte-pair-encoding (BPE) tokenizer. GPT and open models (DeepSeek, Qwen, Llama, Mistral, GLM) tokenize live in your browser and show the actual pieces; Claude's count comes from Anthropic's count_tokens API (it exposes the count, not the pieces).
